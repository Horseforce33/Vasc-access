Why This Course?

Advanced Vascular Access is a practical, evidence-based course designed to provide clinicians with ready-to-use skills for vascular access.

We've sourced expertise from across the North of England including medical consultants and industry experts to provide the highest quality training for your career.

  • Ultrasound-guided insertion principles
  • Evidence-based patient selection
  • Complication prevention & troubleshooting
  • Designed for multidisciplinary clinicians

This short e-learning course provides structured training in vascular access techniques.
